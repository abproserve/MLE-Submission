📘 What is an MLE?

A Multi-Legal Entity (MLE) setup allows a single Amazon Business account to operate with multiple legal entities — such as divisions, subsidiaries, or cost centers — under one umbrella.

Each Legal Entity gets its own billing profile and can have its own Pay By Invoice (PBI) credit line.

✨ Benefits of MLEs

  • Separate invoicing per division or subsidiary
  • Individual Lines of Credit (LOC) per LE
  • Centralized purchasing visibility for the parent account
  • Streamlined compliance and cost allocation
  • Each LE can have its own payment terms
  • Simplified onboarding for large organizations

✅ Before You Start — Checklist

  • ✓ Customer has an active Amazon Business account
  • ✓ You have the Salesforce Opportunity URL/ID
  • ✓ You know the admin email(s) on the account (must be active admins)
  • ✓ You have the list of Legal Entity names to create
  • ✓ You know whether PBI is required
  • ✓ If PBI: LOC amount and Net Terms are confirmed with the customer
  • ✓ No duplicate LE names already exist on the account
  • ✓ All LEs are for the same marketplace (US, CA, or MX)
